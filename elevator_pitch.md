MYLA is a Solana-native mobile dapp that turns every minute of live sports into a micro-prediction market you can trade and settle instantly with a single biometric tap.

Traditional sports betting is slow and passive—you bet hours in advance and wait for the final whistle. MYLA changes that by creating high-frequency, 5-to-15 minute micro-prediction markets during live games—like 'Who wins the next corner?' or 'Will there be a card in the next 10 minutes?'

"By leveraging Solana Mobile's hardware-secured biometric authorization and ultra-low fees, we've made predicting live events as frictionless as using Apple Pay, with payouts settled on-chain the second the play happens.

# MYLA — Make Your Live Assessment

> A real-time micro-prediction game for the Solana Seeker, powered by TxODDS live World Cup data.

**Hackathon:** [Superteam World Cup Hackathon](https://superteam.fun/earn/hackathon/world-cup)  
**Track:** [Trading Tools & Agents](https://superteam.fun/earn/listing/trading-tools-and-agents) ($16,000 USDT)  
**Dates:** June 24 – July 19, 2026  
**Built for:** Solana Seeker dapp store (React Native + Solana Mobile Stack)

---

## 🎯 The Idea

MYLA turns every minute of a live World Cup match into a micro-prediction you can stake on.

While the match is on, short-rolling markets open every 5–30 minutes — "Who gets the next corner?", "Goal in the next 15 min?", "Next yellow card to Team A or B?" Fans tap their predictions, stake small SOL, and watch odds shift in real-time via TxODDS. Markets auto-resolve the moment the event happens.

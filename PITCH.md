# MYLA — Make Your Live Assessment

## 🏆 Superteam World Cup Hackathon | Track 3: Trading Tools & Agents

---

## The One-Liner

**MYLA turns every minute of a live World Cup match into a prediction you can stake on — resolved in real-time via TxODDS data.**

---

## The Problem

Watching a World Cup match is exciting, but the experience is passive. Traditional sports betting requires predicting outcomes hours or days in advance and waiting until the final whistle. During the 90 minutes of play, fans have no way to engage with the action in short, meaningful cycles.

Existing prediction markets are:
- ⏱ **Too slow** — Markets last hours or days
- 💻 **Desktop-first** — Not optimized for mobile, let alone the Seeker
- 😴 **Passive** — No real-time feedback loop with the match

## The Solution

MYLA is a **mobile micro-prediction game** that opens, manages, and resolves prediction markets in 5–30 minute cycles during live World Cup matches.

### How it works

1. **Pick a match** — Browse live World Cup fixtures
2. **Swipe the feed** — See active micro-markets as scrollable cards
3. **Tap to predict** — Choose your outcome (e.g., "Next corner: Brazil or Croatia?")
4. **Confirm with biometrics** — One-tap on Seeker's hardware
5. **Watch the odds move** — Real-time probability shifts with every match event
6. **Win instantly** — Market resolves as soon as the event happens — winnings auto-credited

### Market examples

| Market | Duration | Why it's fun |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| "Will a goal be scored in the next 15 min?" | 15 min | Every near-miss raises tension |
| "Which team gets the next corner?" | ~8 min | Turns a routine play into a betting moment |
| "Total goals over/under 0.5 by halftime?" | 15-45 min | Strategic, rewards match knowledge |
| "Next yellow card — Team A or B?" | ~20 min | Adds stakes to every tackle |

## Why We Win

### 🎯 Track 3 Fit (Trading Tools & Agents)
MYLA is a **fan-facing agent** that ingests TxODDS live odds and scores, detects marketable match events (goals, corners, cards), generates and prices micro-markets, and settles them automatically — all without manual input.

### ⚡ Real-Time by Design
TxODDS' 12ms feed latency means odds update as the ball moves. The UI is built for split-second decisions during live play.

### 🔗 On-Chain Integrity
Every market resolves via TxODDS Tx LINE verifiable data on Solana. Trustless, transparent, instant.

### 📱 Seeker First
Optimized for Solana Mobile Stack — biometric tap-to-confirm, hardware wallet, push notifications. First mover on Seeker's dapp store.

### 🌍 Built for the Moment
The 2026 World Cup is happening NOW (June 11 – July 19). We're not building for a hypothetical future — we're building for tonight's match.
